Research site A

The first site I found to be useful for my project is Coalition for the Homeless. It features all of the aspect of homelessness in the city, helpful links for more information and also tips to better assist the homeless population of New York City.

The Coalition for the Homeless is an organization that advocate for homeless men, women and children since 1981. They offer a variety of programs such as:

  • Housing
  • Job Training
  • Youth Programs
  • Crisis Services
  • And Advocacy

They help the ones that cannot help themselves. The “Get Help” option on the official website allow homeless people to seek help, whether you have been evicted or just hungry, the Coalition for the homeless provide you with food and shelter. They monitor many shelters around the city and make sure less homeless people are sleeping in the street and subway system.
