We Stand for those who can't

For my final project, I would like to create a website that represents the voice of the homeless population, create a platform where people can learn about the reality that homeless people face everyday. The site will inform people on how they can actually help homeless people they encounter everyday, whether by providing them with food or warm clothes to stay warm during the cold weather. My major issue is how to create a fully responsive website. I would add some media queries and with JQuery and bootstrap I believe I can achieve a well designed responsive site across all screens.
