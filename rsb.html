Research site B

The site is the official NYC Department of Homeless Services, it has information about how to help homeless people, where to receive help as a homeless and addresses to many shelters around the city. The Department of Homeless Services is the branch of the HRA (Human Resources Administration) dedicated to assist the homeless population. They provide shelters locations where homeless can reside temporarily while assisting with the process of acquiring a permanent housing. They have an outreach team that travel around the city looking for homeless people in need of assistance. They provide shelter for single adults and shelters for family. Their assistance is not limited only to homeless people; they also assist veterans who are having a hard timing regaining their part in society. The DHS provide information about homelessness prevention, services to prevent eviction, emergency rental assistance, short term financial assistance and also provide assistance for people in need of relocation.
